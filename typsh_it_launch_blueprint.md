# typsh.it Launch Blueprint

## Store Positioning
**Brand:** typsh.it  
**Tagline:** Useful Finds That Actually Make Life Easier  
**Concept:** A clean, modern gadget store focused on visually demo-friendly products that solve everyday problems at home and in tech.

## Brand Direction
- **Style:** clean, minimal, modern tech, premium gadget
- **Tone:** crisp, helpful, confident, not gimmicky
- **Homepage focus:** problem-solving products
- **Primary market:** United States only at launch
- **Payments:** Shopify Payments + PayPal
- **Shipping:** free shipping built into price
- **Support:** support@typsh.it

## Visual Identity
### Logo direction
Use a sleek wordmark: **typsh.it**
- Sans-serif
- Slightly widened tracking
- Optional emphasis on `.it`
- Dark mode and light mode variants

### Color palette
- **Background:** #0F1115
- **Surface:** #161A22
- **Primary text:** #F7F8FA
- **Secondary text:** #A9B1BC
- **Accent:** #3B82F6
- **Soft border:** #252B36

### Typography
- **Headings:** clean geometric sans
- **Body:** highly readable sans
- **Buttons:** medium weight, tight padding, strong contrast

## Suggested Pricing Strategy
- Use **free shipping** and bake fulfillment into price
- Use **compare-at pricing** conservatively
- Use **bundle discounts** for AOV
- Keep one clear hero product and a few add-ons

### Suggested pricing bands
- Drain protector: low-ticket add-on
- Ring light: low/mid-ticket
- Wireless charging station: mid-ticket
- Magnetic screen door: mid-ticket
- Handheld clothes steamer: hero product / premium anchor

## Bundle Strategy
- **Home Refresh Bundle:** steamer + drain protector
- **Desk Setup Bundle:** wireless charging station + ring light
- **Everyday Fixes Bundle:** magnetic screen door + drain protector

## SEO & AEO Build Standard
### SEO
- Unique product titles and meta descriptions
- Optimized collection copy
- Alt text on all product imagery
- Internal links between products, collections, and FAQs
- Indexed policy and contact pages where helpful
- Search Console setup
- Sitemap submission

### AEO
- FAQ sections on products and homepage
- Short answer-first product descriptions
- Clear shipping and return answers on-page
- Problem/solution structure
- Consistent brand and contact details
- Comparison-style benefit bullets where useful
