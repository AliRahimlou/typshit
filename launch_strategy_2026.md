# typsh.it Launch Strategy 2026

## Brand Direction

- Store: typsh.it
- Tagline: Useful Finds That Actually Make Life Easier
- Vibe: clean/minimal, modern tech, TikTok-trendy, premium gadget
- Homepage focus: problem-solving products with home + tech secondary
- Logo: replace immediately with a clean wordmark for `typsh.it`

## Market and Operations

- Launch market: United States only
- Currency: USD
- Payments: Shopify Payments now, PayPal now, Stripe later if needed
- Shipping: free US shipping built into retail price
- Returns: 30-day damaged/defective support flow
- Support: support@typsh.it
- Return address: 3220 Trickum Rd, Marietta, GA 30066

## Product Strategy

- Launch products:
  - Handheld Clothes Steamer
  - Magnetic Screen Door
  - Silicone Drain Protector
  - Wireless Charging Station
  - Ring Light
- Phase 2: Portable Blender
- Margin approach: compact, perceived-value, short-form-friendly products with shipping built into price and strong bundle potential
- Supplier priority: Zendrop, Spocket, CJ Dropshipping

## Theme and UX Rules

- Keep the domain on typsh.it
- Keep the storefront fast and mobile-first
- Favor HTML/CSS-first sections over heavy JavaScript
- Use responsive images and lazy-load below-the-fold media
- Keep homepage sections focused: hero, trust strip, featured products, categories, hero product, reviews, why typsh.it, FAQ preview, email capture

## SEO and AEO Rules

- Use people-first content, not keyword-stuffed copy
- Every page should clearly answer what it is, who it is for, what problem it solves, shipping expectations, and returns expectations
- Add structured data in JSON-LD where the theme supports it
- Validate with Search Console and Rich Results Test before launch

- Reviews app
- Order tracking app
- Upsell or bundles app
- Email popup
